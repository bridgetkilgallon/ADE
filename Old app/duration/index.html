Duration filter

A filter that convert an integer into formatted hours and minutes.

  • Duration "360": {{ 360 | duration }}
  • Invalid duration "abcd": {{ "abcd" | duration }}

Duration editing directive

A directive to edit a time duration string. You can enter time in hours, minutes or a combination.

The directive will broadcast a message when the edit starts and when it finishes so that your controllers and models can handle the edit properly.
